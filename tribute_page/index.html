Emily Jean Stone


About Emily Stones Career and Life

Short Biography:

Emily Jean "Emma" Stone (born November 6, 1988) is an American actress and producer. She began acting in theater as a child, then moved to Los Angeles as a teenager to pursue television and film.
Her breakthrough came with Easy A (2010), followed by acclaimed roles in The Help (2011) and The Amazing Spider-Man series (2012–2014).
She won two Academy Awards for Best Actress for La la land (2016) and Poor Things (2023), also earning praise for Birdman (2014), The Favourite (2018), and Cruella (2021). Beyond film, she has appeared on Broadway in Cabaret and in TV shows like Maniac (2018) and The Curse (2023).
In 2020, she co-founded the production company Fruit Tree with her husband, Dave McCary.
